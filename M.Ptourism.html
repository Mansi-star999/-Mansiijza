About Madhya Pradesh

Located in the heart of India, Madhya Pradesh is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments, and natural beauty. The state offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers, from ancient temples and forts to national parks and wildlife sanctuaries.

With its central location, Madhya Pradesh has been influenced by various cultures and dynasties throughout history, resulting in a unique blend of traditions, art, and architecture.

State Highlights

  • Home to 3 UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • India's largest state by area
  • Known as the "Tiger State" of India
  • Rich tribal culture and heritage

Popular Destinations

Khajuraho

Famous for its stunning temples with intricate carvings, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sanchi Stupa

One of India's oldest stone structures and an important Buddhist monument.

Bandhavgarh National Park

Known for having one of the highest densities of Bengal tigers in India.

Ujjain

One of India's seven sacred cities with the famous Mahakaleshwar Temple.
